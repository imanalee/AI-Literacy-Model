About Us

As a student with access to quality education, I realized that many children do not share the same privilege. They lack resources, awareness, and opportunities to learn and grow in today’s rapidly advancing world. This inspired us to create the AI Literacy Model — an initiative designed to bridge this gap and provide knowledge that truly empowers.

Mission

To raise awareness about artificial intelligence and make educational resources accessible for everyone, reducing inequality in learning opportunities.

Vision & Goals

Our vision is to empower children by providing accessible, high-quality educational resources, enabling them to build a brighter, sustainable future.
